IT'S A YEAR FOR
NEW EXPERIENCES!

It is the year of experiment! After a successful edition of Synergy, we are ready to step out of our tech centric cocoon and venture out in a whole new concept. We at International Organisation of Software Developers intend to bridge the gap between the talented pool of student interns and companies through an Internship Fair as a part of Synergy 2.0. The fair will be a perfect platform for students to meet potential
employers, create networks and gain
challenging opportunities to work and learn.

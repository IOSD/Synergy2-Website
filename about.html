Frequently Asked Questions

Who organizes Synergy 2.0 ?
Synergy 2.0 is organised by the International Organisation of Software Developers, a student organisation pledged to enrich the development culture in the country.
How to register for events under Synergy ?
The registration link will open soon for HackDTU and Code in Less. Follow our Facebook Page to stay updated.
What type of events are HackDTU and Code in Less?

HackDTU is a team event where a maximum of 4 hackers can form a single team. But, you are free to register individually. Also, if you need a team, we will pair you up with other individuals

Code In Less is a team coding competition to test your optimization skills where the shortest and most precise code wins!

How will the events be judged?
You can browse to respective events' pages to find rule books. All rules pertaining to a specific event are listed in the rule book.
